On this page I keep track of my reading / learning. Apart from books on the craft of coding / tech, if you look for book recommendations Tim Ferriss podcast is a good place to start. If you want to recommend me a title feel free to ping me on Twitter.

If you want to roll your own bookshelf, start adding books on FBreadinglist then load http://fbreadinglist.com/bookshelf.php?id=YOUR_FACEBOOK_ID in an iframe on your site. Alternatively you can use Bookshelf creator to generate raw HTML/CSS (no iframe).
