On this page I keep track of my reading / learning. I use an old FB/PHP app (TODO: rewrite in Python/Django) called fbreadinglist. Once you have a few titles there you can iframe a bookshelf on your site using this URL: http://projects.bobbelderbos.com/fbreadinglist/bookshelf.php?id=YOUR_FACEBOOK_ID

Apart from books on the craft of coding / tech, if you are looking for book recommendations check ones mentioned on The Tim Ferriss Show. Another great place to get book recommendations is The Ryan Holiday Reading Recommendation Email.
